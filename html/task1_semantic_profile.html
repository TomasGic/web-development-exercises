About Me

Bio

I am a multilingual aspiring full stack software developer based in Utrecht, Netherlands. I started by programming journey while working as a digital content editor in 2021 when I realised that a lot of workflows and processes in our content solutions department were slow and repetitive and could be automated with code. I first started to learn the basics of HTML and CSS and soon also discovered Python, Javascript and its more Google-centered version Google Apps Script. Using primarily Google Apps Script and Python I automated more than 30 manual workflows and saved our content teams more than 1000 hours of manual work annually.

I am currently participating in a mentorship to transition from pure automation scripting into full-stack software development. I am documenting my learning journey in my Github profile.

In my free time I like to play guitar, play tennis, go running, and read popular science books.
